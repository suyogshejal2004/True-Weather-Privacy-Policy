True Weather App

Privacy Policy & Terms of Use

Last Updated: January 2025


1. Introduction

True Weather ("we", "our", "the app") is designed to provide accurate real-time weather information based on your current location. We respect your privacy and are committed to being transparent about how we handle your data.

2. Permissions We Request

The True Weather app requires the following permissions:

  • Location Permission – used ONLY to fetch weather data for your current location. We do not store or share your location.
  • Notification Permission – used to send weather alerts, warnings, and updates.

3. Information We Collect

The app does NOT collect:

  • No personal information
  • No account details
  • No device identifiers
  • No background data
  • No analytics or tracking

We only temporarily access your device’s GPS location to show weather details. This location data is NOT stored, saved, transferred, or shared.

4. How We Use Your Data

Your location is used solely for:

  • Displaying current weather
  • Showing hourly & weekly forecasts
  • Sending emergency weather notifications (only if enabled)

Your data is never used for marketing, advertising, or analytics.

5. Third-Party Services

True Weather uses trusted third-party weather APIs to fetch weather data. These services receive only your **approximate location coordinates**, but no personal details.

6. Data Security

Since we do not store any user data, there is no risk of data leakage or misuse from our side. All communications with weather servers are protected using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS).

7. Children’s Privacy

True Weather is safe for all ages. It does not collect any personally identifiable information.

8. Your Choices

You can revoke location or notification permission at any time from your device settings. Without location permission, the app may not function correctly.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be shown on this page.
