True Weather — Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters. We collect only what we must.

Location AdMob Ads Secure • HTTPS Encrypted

1. What we collect

True Weather collects only the essential data needed for core functionality and ads.

  • Location (Precise/Approx.) — Used ONLY to show accurate local weather.
  • Ad-related info — Google AdMob may access device signals like Advertising ID.
  • We do not collect photos, contacts, messages, or personal account info.

2. How your data is used

We use information only for:

  • Fetching accurate weather for your current location
  • Showing relevant ads through Google AdMob
  • Improving app performance and reliability
We never sell or trade your data — ever.

3. Third-party services

We only use trusted, secure providers:

  • Weather APIs — Receive only latitude/longitude for weather requests.
  • Google AdMob — Handles ads and may use the Advertising ID for personalization.

4. Data sharing & encryption

The app uses HTTPS encryption for all requests. Data shared with APIs or AdMob is the minimum required.

  • No user accounts → No personal details stored.
  • No cloud storage → No retained personal data.
  • Location is processed ephemerally (not saved).

5. Children’s Privacy

True Weather is safe for general use. Since ads are displayed, parental guidance is recommended for young children.

6. About Advertising ID

AdMob may use the device Advertising ID for:

  • Ad personalization
  • Fraud prevention
  • Ad effectiveness measurement

You may opt out via your device's Ad Personalization settings.

7. Policy Updates

Any updates will be posted here with the updated date at the top.

8. Contact Us

Email us anytime if you have privacy questions.
